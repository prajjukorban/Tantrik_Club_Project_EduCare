Our Story

It all started with a simple question: “Which video should I watch to learn Python?” Like many students, Prachul was lost in a sea of tutorials — too many options, no clear path.

One day, Prajwal posted a message in his college tech club, Tantrik, sharing his idea for a platform where students could easily find quality videos for learning tech skills. Most stayed silent — but a few enthusiastic students responded.

Together, they built a website — a place where anyone can select a course like Python, click a button, and instantly get a curated video to start learning. No confusion. No noise. Just clear learning.

This isn't just a site — it's a mission. Built by learners, for learners.
